Forecasting Future Poverty Levels through Socioeconomic Indicators

As part of my IBM CSRBox SkillsBuild virtual internship, I undertook a project titled “Forecasting Future Poverty Levels through Socioeconomic Indicators.” This initiative focused on creating predictive models that utilize historical socioeconomic data to identify potential poverty trends and recommend actionable solutions in line with Sustainable Development Goal 1 (No Poverty). By leveraging Python, Scikit-learn, and Tableau, I examined critical indicators such as GDP, unemployment rates, and access to education to uncover insights that can inform data-driven policy-making and enhance poverty alleviation efforts.
